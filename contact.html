Got questions?

Frequently Asked

How do I place an order?
You can order directly through our website by adding items to your cart and checking out via WhatsApp. For custom wigs, use our Custom Wig Studio form.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept EcoCash, InnBucks, bank transfer (Zimbabwe), and international payments via PayPal and Wise. Payment details are provided on WhatsApp after order confirmation.
How long does shipping take?
Local delivery (Harare): 1-2 days. Zimbabwe inter-city: 2-4 days. International: 7-14 days depending on location. Tracking provided for all orders.
Can I return or exchange a wig?
Due to hygiene reasons, wigs cannot be returned once worn. However, if there's a manufacturing defect, contact us within 48 hours of delivery for resolution.
How do I care for my Her Haven wig?
We provide care instructions with every order. Use sulfate-free shampoo, cold water, and air dry. Store on a mannequin head to maintain shape.
Do you offer installation services?
Yes, we offer professional wig installation and styling services at our Harare studio. Bookings are required in advance.
