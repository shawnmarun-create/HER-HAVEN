Our Story

Home · About
Dedicated to her glow

A haven built for the woman who wants more than just hair.

Her Haven started as a quiet obsession with detail — the kind of detail you only notice when a wig fits like it was made for you, because it was. Founded in Harare, Zimbabwe, we set out to change the hair industry by offering something truly different: ethically sourced raw hair, hand-crafted wigs, and a personal touch that mass production can never replicate.

Today we serve queens across Zimbabwe, South Africa, the UK, USA, and beyond. Every bundle is single-donor raw hair. Every wig is hand-bleached, toned and customized by our in-studio team. And every order is finished with the same care we'd give our own crowns.

We don't just sell hair. We deliver confidence, one custom piece at a time.

— The Her Haven Team
