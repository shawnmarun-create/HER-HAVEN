Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does a custom wig take?
Our custom wigs typically take 7-14 days to complete, depending on complexity and our current order volume. Rush orders available for an additional fee.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship worldwide from Harare, Zimbabwe. Delivery times vary by location (3-10 days for Africa, 7-14 days for international).
Can I send a reference photo?
Absolutely! Include reference images in the notes section or send them directly on WhatsApp after submitting your order.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept EcoCash, InnBucks, bank transfer, and international payments via PayPal/Wise. Payment details provided on WhatsApp.
